WWDC2024 Action Pack

Apple's Worldwide Developers Conference (WWDC) 2024 was a week-long online event that featured new software updates, emerging technologies, and other announcements. Some of the key takeaways from WWDC 2024 include: Apple Intelligence: A personal intelligence system that uses AI and machine learning to enable personalized interactions and advanced content generation. Apple Intelligence will be available on many Apple devices, including the iPad Pro, iPad Air, MacBook Pro, MacBook Air, iMac, Mac Mini, Mac Studio, and Mac Pro. Enhanced messaging: RCS will provide enhanced messaging capabilities. iPhone push notifications: iPhone push notifications will now be mirrored on Macs. Game Porting Toolkit 2: This tool will make gaming even better. Apple Vision Pro: Apple Vision Pro will go global. Live Activities: Live Activities will come to Apple Watch. Enhanced privacy: Apps will have enhanced privacy. Siri: Siri will have more capabilities powered by AI and machine learning. WWDC is an annual conference that Apple hosts at its headquarters in Cupertino, California. The event includes a keynote, Platforms State of the Union, sessions, labs, and forums.
